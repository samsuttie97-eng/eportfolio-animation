Digital Animation

Animated learning with Adobe Animate

Using Adobe Animate, I learned how animation can simplify complex ideas by combining movement, visuals, and narration to teach scientific topics effectively.

Educational Animation

I created a brief animation in Adobe Animate explaining why chameleons change color. Using keyframes, motion tweens, and layered visuals, I illustrated how chameleons adapt their skin tone for camouflage. The project combined simple animation techniques with scientific concepts to make the topic engaging and easy to understand.
